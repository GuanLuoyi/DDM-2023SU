SCENE2-SPACE

The intense animated 'noisescape' of urban events can be experienced as an exhilarating alternative to traditional scenic views. The 'noisescape' visually resembles a mixture of video noise and a high-tech construction site, but it's alse suggestive of natural phenomena such as trees blowing in the wind or the sound of rain. Various configurations of perforated dividers and noise-sheidling enclosures are deployed to create spaces with different kinds of exposure to the turbulent and dramatic environment. Portable custom electronic audio-visual display devices are used to isolate or amplify particular noise features-- like tuning a radio to a favorite station.
