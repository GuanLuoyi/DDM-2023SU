DEMO DWELLING MACHINES

SCENE1-LIGHT

Scene is like a giant stage set. Some backdrop elements are fixed on giant billboard, others can be slid in out on from drawers in a giant filing cabinet. Giant moving lights (artificial suns) of different scales and on different kinds of rail. Mechanical foliage, devices which look like radically abstract mechanized hedges. can be adjusted to let more or less light pass through. Although a very industrial aethetic, the feeling is luxurious and opulent. a mix of raw infrastructure/machinery/metal and fancy materials like marble.
